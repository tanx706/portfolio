Summary

I am a Principal Lighting Artist dedicated to creating immersive, hyper-realistic environments and look development for architecture and high-end gaming. My work is defined by a mastery of visual storytelling, mood creation, and cinematic composition within real-time engines, with a specialized focus on Unreal Engine 5 and procedural workflows.

I lead the artistic direction of lighting by establishing rigorous style guides, color palettes, and mood boards. I specialize in polishing environments to a cinematic standard while ensuring gameplay readability and player guidance remain paramount. By blending artistic sensitivity with deep technical knowledge, I leverage modern rendering technologies—including Lumen, Nanite, Volumetric Clouds/Fog, and Dynamic Exposure—to create atmospheric worlds that are emotionally impactful and technically seamless.

Optimization & Leadership: I prioritize lighting performance by meticulously managing light complexity and radius overlaps. As a mentor, I focus on pushing the artistic growth of the team, setting quality benchmarks, and ensuring that lighting consistently communicates a cohesive story across all levels.

Forest Lighting Study

Unreal Engine 5.3 | Lumen | Nanite

This project focuses on hyper-realistic lighting and atmospheric fog within a dense forest environment. Utilizing Lumen for real-time global illumination and Nanite for high-fidelity geometry, I aimed to capture the "God Ray" effect during the morning golden hour.
